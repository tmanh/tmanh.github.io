Application for Permission to Engage in Activity other than that Permitted under the Status of Residence Previously Granted

2018-05-05

Why?

If you want to have a part-time job in Japan to pay for living expenses, tuition fee or something else, you have to submit the "Application for Permission to Engage in Activity other than that Permitted under the Status of Residence Previously Granted" to the Kanazawa Immigration Bureau (It's free and if you do not know how to get there, please look at this ). Then, you will receive a stamp as below

Notice: You cannot have any part-time jobs without the permission.

Preparation

Necessary documents

  • Application form 1 copy ( here )
  • Passport and residence card (or alien registration certificate deemed equivalent to a residence card)
  • A document that proves you are a student (remember to bring your Student Identity Card)

Office hours

9:00 to 12:00 and 13:00 to 16:00, weekdays

For more information, please visit this webpage of Immigration Bureau of Japan. Officially, the procedures duration is 2-8 weeks. However, it only takes few minutes for me to get the stamp on my residence card.
